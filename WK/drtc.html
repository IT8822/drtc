About Tyler, The Creator

Tyler, The Creator is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. He rose to prominence as the leader of the alternative hip hop collective Odd Future and has since built a successful solo career. His unique blend of creativity, controversial lyrics, and genre-bending music have made him one of the most influential artists of his generation.

Known for albums like *Goblin*, *Wolf*, and *Igor*, Tyler continues to evolve as both an artist and a cultural icon, blending elements of jazz, R&B, soul, and hip hop into his work.
